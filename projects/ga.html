Projects / Google Analytics API and Google Charts

This is a project currently in use in my organisation. It follows (as ever) some similar experiments by other LocalGovDigital peers

The charts below are generated by a set of Google Charts in a Google Sheet, which uses the Google Analytics Add-on to pull through live data from our Analytics feed and filter/report as required. This runs each morning to update the data

A nice low-code and useful product to play with. I'm looking to expand the use of embedded Google Charts to try and cut down on the number of PDF reports we upload to our intranet every month; better for the reader and easier to maintain for the user.
